Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: April 27, 2025

1. Return Window

Customers may request a return within 1 day of receiving their purchase.

2. Eligible Products

Returns are accepted only for unused and unopened products. Products that have been opened or used are not eligible for return.

3. Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for the shipping costs associated with returning a product. We recommend using a trackable shipping service for returns.

4. Refunds and Service Costs

Due to service charges associated with processing returns, customers will not receive a full refund. The refunded amount will reflect deductions for applicable service fees.

5. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned under any circumstances:

  • Perishable goods
  • Products clearly marked as "Non-Returnable" or "Final Sale"

6. Damaged or Incorrect Products

If you receive a product that is damaged or incorrect, please contact us immediately. We offer a full refund for damaged or wrong items.

7. Refund Processing Time

Once your return is received and inspected, we will process your refund within 7 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used at checkout.

8. Proof of Purchase

To complete your return, a proof of purchase (such as an order confirmation email or receipt) is required.

9. Contact Information

For any questions or to initiate a return, please contact us:

We appreciate your understanding and support. Thank you for shopping with Betegna!
